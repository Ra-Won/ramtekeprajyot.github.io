Responsibilities
Cover Page Designing
Beginning

Anusmriti is the annual magazine of Indian Institute of Technology Patna released by Alumni Association of the Institute for whole year's updates.

I have been memeber of the Design team of the Association for 2 years and got a chance to design a magazine and learn different aspects of it. As head designer, it was my responsibility to design the cover page for the latest volume (Volume 5). We ideated on what should be on the cover page and then I came up with the idea that we should have our institute gate as it symbolises tha name 'IITP' and everyone would be able to relte to it as it is the first thing that every student would see while coming to campus.

After finalising the idea I illustrated the whole gate using Figma and gave it a pseudo 3D look (wanted to try illustration).

Concluding

Designing Team of the Alumni Association was responsible for designing posters, identity cards, merchandise, etc., along with the magazine. So on a whole it was a good learning experience for every team memeber while working with the Association.

Learnt about editorial typography, color psychology, micro illustrations and minimal designing. Here the cover page signifies the attachment of a student with the institute, embarks thier journey while enetering through this gate and will become a visualisation of all thier time spent in the institute. Colors scheme used was the standard colors set by in the Volume 1 of the magazine.

The design was quite appreciated by everyone and the motive behind the design was fulfilled according the response of readers.
