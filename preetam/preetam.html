Beginning

Preetam Freight Vehicle Service was an established business in Maharasthtra and they wanted to make appliactions for their smooth functioning.

As of now I was having experience in Graphic & User Experience Designing and wanted to explore Animation so I took up this job for making a 3D Animated launch video for the client.

This idea behind this video was to explain the working of the application and gain maximum number of users for the client. We tried to use visuals so tha users would be able to relate easily.

Concluding

This was my first project where I got to work with 3D modelleing and animation. Client was satisfied by the output and response of audience was also quite good.

On a whole I was able to learn Blender 3D and animation basics, keyframes, light positioning, camera movement, and audio editing. The application would be having two major user, vehicle drivers and consumers who would be booking vehicles, this video was targeted for the consumers and one more video was launched later for drivers too. The established service was working with regional drivers so the application provided a multi-lingual facility for their ease.

Got to know that an animation project might also have user experience ideas and we should think from perspective of our target audience.
